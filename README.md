# -Optimizing-Supply-Chain-Efficiency-Inventory-Performance-Dashboard

I’m excited to share my latest Inventory Performance & Optimization Dashboard, designed to bridge the gap between warehouse operations and financial health. 📊
Effective inventory management isn't just about counting boxes—it’s about balancing Capital vs. Service Levels. This dashboard utilizes ABC/XYZ analysis to pinpoint exactly where capital is trapped and where service risks are lurking.
Key Highlights from the Analysis:
⚠️ The "Obsolete" Trap: Identified that 49.2% of SKUs are currently stagnant (90+ days), signaling a critical need for aggressive liquidation to free up warehouse capacity.
📦 Backorder Bottlenecks: Uncovered a massive 298K unit shortfall in low-value items. While individually "cheap," these gaps cause major ripples in customer satisfaction.
💰 Capital Allocation: Visualized a $3.2B inventory portfolio, highlighting that high-value "A" items with uniform demand are the primary drivers of investment.
📉 Excess Mitigation: Targeted $300M in excess inventory specifically within "Uncertain" demand categories to streamline cash flow.
Turning complex data into actionable procurement strategies is the key to a resilient supply chain. 🛠️
